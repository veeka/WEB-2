HUMANS OF NEW YORK

Brandon Stanton began Humans of New York in the summer of 2010. I thought it would be really cool to create an exhaustive
catalogue of New York City’s inhabitants, so I set out to photograph 10,000 New Yorkers and plot their photos on a map.
I worked for several months with this goal in mind, but somewhere along the way. I started collecting quotes and short stories
from the people I met, and began including these snippets alongside the photographs. Taken together, these portraits and
captions became the subject of a vibrant blog. With over eight million followers on social media.

It has also become a #1 NYT bestselling book.. It’s been quite a ride so far. Feel free to follow along.
